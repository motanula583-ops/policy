Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 14, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Elvoria ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot or website. We are committed to collecting only the minimum data required for functionality.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following limited information:

  • Discord User IDs & Server (Guild) IDs: Used for configuration, premium status, and moderation logic.
  • Command Usage Data: Used for debugging, performance monitoring, and abuse prevention.
  • Premium Status Information: Stored to determine access to premium features or trials.
  • User-Saved Data: Such as song favorites or preferences explicitly saved by users.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Passwords, email addresses, or private messages
  • Voice recordings or live voice data
  • Cross-site or advertising tracking data

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain core bot functionality
  • To manage premium access and feature eligibility
  • To diagnose errors, outages, and performance issues
  • To protect against spam, abuse, or malicious activity

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. Data may be processed by:

  • Discord: In accordance with Discord’s Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare: For website delivery, security, and traffic handling

6. Third-Party Services

Elvoria may interact with third-party APIs and services such as Spotify, SoundCloud, and other audio or metadata providers. These services operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

We do not control how third-party services collect, use, or store data. Your use of those services is governed solely by their respective privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to operate the service. Server-specific data may be removed automatically when the bot is removed or manually upon request.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Your Rights

You may request deletion of stored data associated with your user or server by contacting us through our support server.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of Elvoria constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, please join our official support server .
